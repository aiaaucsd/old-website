Outreach

Volunteers needed

We need your help! AIAA @ UCSD has worked with Triton Engineering Student Council and many local organizations in San Diego to bring forth outreach and volunteering events, inspiring young kids to join the forces of engineering and scientific work tomorrow. Please check the Events page for the most up-to-date volunteering opportunities.

We're here to help

AIAA @ UCSD is always looking for new opportunities to serve the local community and inspire kids to pursue careers in engineering and science. If you're looking for college students to host interactive presentations for pupils and to talk about college life and career opportunities as engineers, feel free to contact us.
