About Us

Who Are We?

The American Institute of Aernautics and Astronautics, is a global community striving to engage, foster and promote professionalism, education, communication, and innovation of aeronautical and astronautical endeavors. The University of California San Diego student chapter of AIAA is one of over 160 student branches devoted towards the fulfillment of this goal.

Scholarships

Reuben H. Fleet Scholarship

Every year, the San Diego AIAA Section (AIAA-SD) selects high school and college students (from UCSD and SDSU) to receive the Reuben H. Fleet Scholarship. Deadline to apply is April 2015. More information can be found on the AIAA-SD RHF Scholarship page.
