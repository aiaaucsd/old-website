Scholarships

Reuben H. Fleet Scholarship

Every year, the San Diego AIAA Section (AIAA-SD) selects high school and college students (from UCSD and SDSU) to receive the Reuben H. Fleet Scholarship. Deadline to apply is April. More information can be found on the AIAA-SD RHF Scholarship page.



National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) Scholarships

The National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) is the United States government agency that is responsible for the civilian space program as well as for aeronautics and aerospace research. Towards this end, NASA provides a number of scholarships, internship opportunities, and fellowship opportunities for undergraduate and graduate students. More information on the scholarship, internship, and fellowship opportunities can be found at the links above.

Internship Tips

The key to securing an internship is applying early. Many companies open their internship requisitions online in November, and start filling positions soon after. Be prepared by following these easy steps:

  • Keep an updated resume
  • Update your company profile page often
  • Set a job search agent to help find internships that fit your interests
  • Apply early, apply often! It can take as many as 40 applications to secure one interview
