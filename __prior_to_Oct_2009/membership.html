membership
Who can join and how? Membership is open to all UCSD students regardless of major. All you have to do is subscribe to our e-mail list and show up to events.

To subscribe to our e-mail list, visit this page.

Membership fees? Event fees? Why, no! It is free to attend all AIAA at UCSD meeting and events unless noted. However, a National Student Membership is necessary to attend conferences and compete in DBF. It is also highly encouraged that members join AIAA National for a nominal fee of $20.

Why should I join AIAA National?
  • $2000 Scholarships for sophomores, juniors, and seniors - 30 to choose from
  • Network opportunities with industry leaders
  • Student discounts on professional conferences, local section events, and technical meetings
  • Member discounts - 30-40% off AIAA textbooks
  • The ability to participate in competitions with cash prizes
  • A free magazine subscription to Aerospace America and the AIAA Student Journal
To sign up for national AIAA and to find out more, click here.

If you have any other questions regarding membership, please contact Julia Stalder, Chairperson of AIAA@UCSD, at jstalder@ucsd.edu.
