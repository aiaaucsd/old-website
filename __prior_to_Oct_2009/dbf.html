ñ 2005/2006 design/build/fly (DBF)
We placed 9th out of about 50 teams - beating all three MIT teams, SDSU, UCLA and 35 other schools! Students designed, fabricated, and demonstrated the flight capabilities of an unmanned, electric-powered, radio-controlled aircraft meeting specifications outlined by Cessna/ONR.
