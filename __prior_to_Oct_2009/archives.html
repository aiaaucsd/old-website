i upcoming events and news
JPL Info Session and Interviews
Monday, October 16 - Tuesday, October 17, 2006
Time of both events will be announced.

JPL is searching for entry level students majoring in Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Physics, Mechanical Engineering, Aerospace, and/or Systems. The info session will be held on Monday; interviews will be conducted on Tuesday. To apply for a position, apply at the Career Services Center by Tuesday, October 3, 2006.

Interested in Submitting a Technical Paper?
The Intelligent Systems Student Paper Competition will be held in conjunction with the Infotech@Aerospace technical program. Papers are sought from graduate and undergraduate students on a host of intelligent systems topics. Finalists will present their papers at a special student paper session, and the best paper author will receive a $1,000 prize and recognition at the conference. All awards are courtesy of BAE Systems.

Details can be found in the Call for Papers (CFP) brochure here.

Please note that the abstract deadline is 14 October 2006. The CFP and all conference information can be found at http://www.aiaa.org/events/infotech@aerospace.
